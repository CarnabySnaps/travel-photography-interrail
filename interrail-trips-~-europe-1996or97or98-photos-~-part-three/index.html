InterRail Trips ~ Europe 1996|97|98 Photos ~ Part Three

Throughout the summers of 1995 to 1998 I InterRailed around the countries of Belgium, Holland, Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Germany, Czech Republic, Hungry, Austria, Switzerland, Italy, Spain & France and here are some of my favourite photo’s that I’ve scanned in for you to see…

Part Three’s photo’s are taken in the citys of Bremen, Cologne & Munster in Germany (for the World Cup Skateboarding event), then onto Prague, Czech Republic, followed by Italy’s famous water city of Venice and then Florence & Verona…
